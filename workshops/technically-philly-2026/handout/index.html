The 3-Layer Model

L1 · Inputs

CRM notes, financials, SOPs, brand — what you already have, scattered across tools.

L2 · Knowledge

Markdown as source of truth. AI maintains structure. The layer most teams skip.

L3 · Decisions

Dashboard, pipeline, finance views — static pages compiled from your knowledge.
