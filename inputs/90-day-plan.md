# 90-Day Plan
**Business:** Design Grove
**Period:** June 2026 – August 2026
**Updated:** 2026-06-04

## North Star
**Identify 10 qualified nonprofit prospects** with a named contact, clear fit, and a planned first outreach by end of August.

## Month 1 — June
**Goal:** Define your ideal client profile and build a researched target list.
- [ ] Write a one-page ICP: org size, mission areas, and signals they need mapping help (owner: You · due: 2026-06-15)
- [ ] Research and list 15–20 candidate nonprofits in your network or region (owner: You · due: 2026-06-22)
- [ ] Draft a short outreach message + 15-minute discovery call offer (owner: You · due: 2026-06-28)

## Month 2 — July
**Goal:** Start conversations and refine who responds.
- [ ] Reach out to first 5 prospects on the list (owner: You · due: 2026-07-10)
- [ ] Hold at least 2 discovery calls and capture pain points + fit notes (owner: You · due: 2026-07-25)
- [ ] Publish one anonymized “process clarity” post or mini case outline for credibility (owner: You · due: 2026-07-31)

## Month 3 — August
**Goal:** Hit 10 identified prospects and prioritize follow-ups.
- [ ] Complete outreach to remaining prospects to reach **10 total** with documented status (owner: You · due: 2026-08-15)
- [ ] Score each prospect (fit, urgency, budget likelihood) and pick top 3 for proposal or workshop pitch (owner: You · due: 2026-08-22)
- [ ] Book at least 1 paid workshop or scoped discovery engagement from the pipeline (owner: You · due: 2026-08-31)

## Bets
1. **LinkedIn + warm intro mix** — Testing whether short posts plus 2–3 personal intros per week beats cold email for nonprofit leaders.
2. **Free 30-minute “map one workflow” offer** — Testing whether a low-commitment teaser converts better than pitching a full workshop upfront.
3. **Association / funder event** — Testing one speaking slot or roundtable to fill 3–5 names on the list faster than solo research.

## Risks
- Summer scheduling slows nonprofit responses — batch outreach early in the week and follow up once.
- “10 businesses” might mean for-profits vs nonprofits — clarify each prospect’s sector in your tracker to avoid wasted outreach.
- Capacity: facilitation time competes with biz dev — block 3–4 hours weekly for list building and outreach only.
