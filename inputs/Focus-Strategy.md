# Strategy & Goals

# ---

## Business Purpose

Design Grove helps organizations improve the way work flows through their systems, services, and teams.

The focus is on uncovering operational friction, clarifying processes, improving collaboration, and designing services that function more effectively for both employees and customers.

Core areas of focus include:

* Service design  
* Process mapping  
* Workshop facilitation  
* Cross-functional alignment  
* Operational improvement  
* Research-informed strategy

My goal is to help organizations better understand how their systems actually operate in practice — not just how they are intended to operate on paper.

## Strategy 

Transition from positioning primarily as a web/UI designer toward positioning as a service design and process improvement consultant.

Primary strategy areas:

* Build confidence and fluency in process mapping facilitation  
* Develop repeatable workshop and documentation methods  
* Use existing UX research background as a foundation for service design work  
* Pursue values-aligned organizations where operational clarity and employee experience matter  
* Create small but credible portfolio artifacts demonstrating facilitation and systems thinking  
* Leverage network connections and collaborative communities to identify opportunities

Web development and UI design remain supporting capabilities, but are no longer the primary business focus.

## Goals

### Long Term (90 days)

- Land 2 high quality client contracts  
- Develop a repeatable process for landing high quality clients

### Short Term (30 days)

- Identify potential values aligned clients that would benefit from this work  
- Develop confidence in providing the core offerings (service and process mapping)
